The Mindanao AIDS Advocates Inc. (MAAAI) commit themselves to (a) prevent sexually transmitted infections (STI), HIV, and AIDS in Mindanao (b) empowers people living with HIV to address STI, HIV, and AIDS issues and concerns in the community, and (c) strengthen the bond between service providers and people living with HIV in Mindanao. They offer help and assistance to STI patients who require assistance not only physically but also mentally. Additionally, they educate others about STIs. Along with care and support, they also offer advocacy, networking, and capacity building.

The Mindanao AIDS Advocates Association established its first office at #557 Kamuning Street, Juna Subd., at the office of ALAGAD Mindanao, Inc. Davao City's Matina. MAAAI has been working at this office for three years. It was then relocated to the 2nd floor of the Reproductive Health and Wellness Center (RHWC) Bldg., Jacinto St., Davao City, and is now part of the SPMC-HACT clinic.
